Tech Stack

Written in JavaScript, e$tymate is built in Reactjs and Redux; bundled by Webpack; served on Node / Express; and its data is managed by MySQL.
e$tymate - a film production budgeting web application - streamlines budgeting for film producers, administrators, and their interns. Its combined use of Reactjs for component-based rendering and Redux for lightning fast, uni-directional data-flow, efficiently automates the cumbersome task of organizing many financial spreadsheets.
